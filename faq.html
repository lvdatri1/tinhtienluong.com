Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about Vietnam salary laws and deduction rules.

What is Personal Deduction?

Every employee in Vietnam is entitled to a personal deduction of 11,000,000 VND per month from their taxable income.

What counts as a Dependent?

Dependents can include children under 18, students with low income, or elderly parents. Each dependent reduces your taxable income by 4,400,000 VND per month.

How much is the Unemployment Benefit?

The monthly benefit is 60% of your average salary of the last 6 months, capped at 5 times the regional minimum wage.

What is the Funeral Allowance?

If an SI participant dies, their family receives 10 times the current base salary for funeral expenses (approx 23.4M VND in 2024).

How long is Unemployment Payout?

3 months for the first 12-36 months of payment, then 1 extra month for every additional 12 months with a maximum of 12 months total.
