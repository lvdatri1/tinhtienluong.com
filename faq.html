Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about Vietnam salary laws and deduction rules.

What is Personal Deduction?

Every employee in Vietnam is entitled to a personal deduction of 11,000,000 VND per month from their taxable income.

What counts as a Dependent?

Dependents can include children under 18, students with low income, or elderly parents. Each dependent reduces your taxable income by 4,400,000 VND per month.

How is PIT calculated?

Personal Income Tax (PIT) is calculated using a progressive table with rates from 5% to 35% based on your taxable income.
